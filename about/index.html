about

Hi, I’m Suhani!

I’m a first-year at Harvard College studying mechanical engineering. I like making things, taking things apart, and figuring out why something works the way it does.

A little more about me

I’m interested in the space between engineering and creativity. Some days that means working on a fabrication project or research question. Other days it means sketching, folding paper, playing music, or writing something down because I want to remember how I thought about it.

I made this website because I wanted one place to put the different things I care about. A resume is useful, but it does not really show the process, the unfinished ideas, or the smaller projects that still matter.

Right now, I’m especially interested in mechanical design, sustainability, hands-on building, and projects that connect technical work with real-world problems.

Currently

  • Studying mechanical engineering at Harvard
  • Learning more fabrication and design tools
  • Working on research and sustainability projects
  • Trying to keep making time for music, art, and writing
